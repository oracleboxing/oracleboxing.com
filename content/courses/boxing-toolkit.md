# Boxing Toolkit

**Type:** Course (Technique Library)
**Thumbnail:** https://sb.oracleboxing.com/courses/thumbnails/boxing-toolkit.webp
**Videos:** 59 technique videos

## Description

59 technique videos covering stance to advanced counters. Every fundamental boxing technique broken down and demonstrated. A reference library you come back to again and again.

## What's Covered

- Stance and guard variations
- All punch types (jab, cross, hooks, uppercuts)
- Defensive techniques (slips, rolls, blocks, parries)
- Footwork patterns
- Combination templates
- Counter-punching setups
- Advanced techniques and counters

## Format

Individual technique breakdown videos. Watch, study, and drill at your own pace. Designed as a reference companion to the grading system and other courses.
