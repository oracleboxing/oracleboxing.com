# Grade 1

**Type:** Grading System (Progressive Curriculum)
**Thumbnail:** https://sb.oracleboxing.com/courses/thumbnails/grade-1.webp
**Access:** Included with membership. First grade - open to all members.
**Prerequisites:** None

## Description

Your boxing journey starts here. Work through the follow-along workouts at your own pace, building the fundamental skills every boxer needs.

## What You Learn

- Basic stance and guard
- Jab and cross mechanics
- Footwork fundamentals
- Weight distribution
- Basic combinations
- Relaxation and form

## Structure

Follow-along workout videos that teach specific skills through guided practice. Complete the curriculum, then book a grading call to demonstrate proficiency and unlock Grade 2.

## Grading Call

At the end of Grade 1, members book a grading assessment call with a coach. The coach evaluates:
- Stance and guard quality
- Jab and cross technique
- Basic footwork
- Overall form and relaxation

**Pass:** Unlocks Grade 2 content in Skool. Receives grading certificate.
**Fail:** Feedback provided. Can reattempt next grading window or book a private grading call ($50).

## Grading Windows

- Monthly group grading sessions (one morning + one evening to cover timezones)
- Private grading calls available anytime: https://cal.com/team/oracle-boxing/level-1-grading-call
