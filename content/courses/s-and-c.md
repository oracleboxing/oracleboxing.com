# S&C (Strength & Conditioning)

**Type:** Course (Training)
**Thumbnail:** https://sb.oracleboxing.com/courses/thumbnails/s-and-c.webp

## Description

Exercise breakdowns and technique to get the most out of them whilst avoiding injury. A comprehensive strength and conditioning program designed specifically for boxers.

## What's Covered

- Exercise breakdowns with proper form
- Injury prevention techniques
- Boxing-specific strength training
- Conditioning protocols
- Mobility and flexibility work
- Recovery strategies

## Purpose

Build the physical foundation that supports your boxing. Proper strength and conditioning prevents injury, increases power, and improves endurance in the ring.
