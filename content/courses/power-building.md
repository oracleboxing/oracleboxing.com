# Power-Building

**Type:** Course (Training)
**Thumbnail:** https://sb.oracleboxing.com/courses/thumbnails/power-building.webp

## Description

Combining the strength gains of powerlifting with the muscle gains of bodybuilding. A hybrid training approach designed for fighters who want to be both strong and athletic.

## What's Covered

- Powerlifting fundamentals (squat, bench, deadlift)
- Bodybuilding hypertrophy principles
- Hybrid programming for fighters
- Periodization and progressive overload
- Exercise selection for boxing performance
- Balancing size and speed

## Purpose

Get stronger and more muscular without sacrificing boxing performance. The power-building approach gives you the best of both worlds - raw strength from powerlifting and the physique development from bodybuilding.
