# Grade 2

**Type:** Grading System (Progressive Curriculum)
**Thumbnail:** https://sb.oracleboxing.com/courses/thumbnails/grade-2.webp
**Access:** Locked. Must pass Grade 1 grading call.
**Prerequisites:** Grade 1 pass

## Description

This course is locked. To access Grade 2, you must pass your Grade 1 grading call. Build on your fundamentals with more advanced combinations and defensive skills.

## What You Learn

- Combinations and punch sequencing
- Defensive movement
- Head movement fundamentals
- Angles and positioning
- Counter-punching basics
- Distance management

## Structure

Follow-along workouts progressing from Grade 1 foundation. More complex drills, defensive integration, and partner work concepts.

## Grading

Pass the Grade 2 grading call to unlock Grade 3. Same format as Grade 1 assessment.
